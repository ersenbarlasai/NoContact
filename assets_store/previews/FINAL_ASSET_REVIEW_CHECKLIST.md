# Final Asset Review Checklist

Before uploading any graphic assets from `assets_store/` to App Store Connect or Google Play Console, stakeholders must verify the following items:

## 1. Icon Review
- [ ] **Readability:** Does the `pause_reflect_icon` concept read clearly when scaled down to a small size (e.g., on a phone home screen)?
- [ ] **Brand Alignment:** Does it strictly avoid dating clichés (no hearts) and medical clichés (no crosses)?
- [ ] **Format Validation:** Does the iOS icon have NO transparency, and is the Android icon correctly formatted?

## 2. Screenshot Visual & Copy Review
- [ ] **Readability:** Are the overlay headlines and captions legible against the Cream/Sage backgrounds?
- [ ] **Device Mockups:** Are the phone frames fully visible (not awkwardly cropped at the top or bottom)?
- [ ] **Localization:** Is ALL visible text inside the phone UI in Turkish? (e.g., "Bugün kendini seçtin" instead of "Morning, Alex").
- [ ] **Data Safety:** Does the mock data avoid looking like real, identifiable user journal entries or letters?

## 3. Store Policy & Claims Review
- [ ] **No Medical Claims:** Ensure the screenshots do not promise "guaranteed healing" or position the app as professional therapy or emergency intervention.
- [ ] **No Artificial AI Claims:** Ensure the Mock AI is not marketed visually as a live production AI system yet.
- [ ] **No Pricing/Free Text:** Screenshots must not contain the words "Free", "Ücretsiz", or pricing details, as per store policies.

## 4. Still Design Consistency
- [ ] **Colors:** Are the assets using only the Still tokens (Sage Green, Cream, Terracotta, Dark Charcoal)?
- [ ] **Vibe:** Do the screenshots and icon convey a sense of calm, stillness, and privacy?
