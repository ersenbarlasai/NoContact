# Brand Name Options

# Important Note
These are preliminary naming ideas. App Store, Google Play, trademark, domain, and social handle checks must be repeated before final selection. A web search on 2026-05-10 showed no obvious same-category app match for the strongest candidates below, but that is not a legal clearance.

# Strong Candidates

## Durula
- Meaning: "become clear/calm" in Turkish.
- Why it fits: recovery, emotional clarity, calm after turbulence.
- Risk: Turkish verb form may be less global, but memorable locally.

## Kalbora
- Meaning: invented brand with "kalp" association.
- Why it fits: soft, brandable, unique-sounding, suitable for global expansion.
- Risk: needs pronunciation testing.

## DinginKalp
- Meaning: calm heart.
- Why it fits: direct Turkish emotional promise.
- Risk: longer name; less global.

## KoruKalp
- Meaning: protect the heart.
- Why it fits: strong SOS/no-contact framing.
- Risk: may sound slightly defensive.

## NefesAra
- Meaning: take a breath / create a pause.
- Why it fits: perfectly aligned with "pause before contact."
- Risk: broader wellness positioning, less breakup-specific.

# Names To Avoid Or Treat Carefully
- SakinKal: similar name exists in Google Play for an OCD/anxiety app.
- Noqta: existing apps and services appear under that name.
- Solacea: existing App Store/product references appear.
- Mendora: existing wellness-like website appears.
- Temassiz/Temassız: too generic and heavily used for contactless payment/commerce.

# My Current Preference
`Durula` for Turkish-first launch, or `Kalbora` if we want a more ownable invented brand. My instinct: start with `Durula` as the product concept and keep `Kalbora` as a backup while legal/store checks are done.
