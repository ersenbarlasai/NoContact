# Still Design System

**Name:** Still
**Personality:** "The Quiet Companion"
**Vibe:** Safe, non-judgmental, emotionally grounding. Warm Minimalist. Visual Silence. High-quality linen journal, calm morning.

## Radius & Spacing

- **Radius:** sm: 4, default: 8, md: 12, lg: 16, xl: 24, full: 9999 (Pill)
- **Spacing:** unit: 8px
- **Margins:** Mobile: 24px, Desktop: 64px
- **Gutter:** 16px
- **Section Gap:** 48px

## Design Philosophy
- **Visual Silence:** Avoid clutter. Use generous negative space.
- **Tonal Layering:** Use surface colors to separate content rather than heavy borders.
- **Warmth:** Sage and Terracotta provide a grounding, human feel.
- **Privacy First:** Design should feel intimate and secure.
- **Low Arousal:** Avoid bright, neon, or high-contrast vibrations.
