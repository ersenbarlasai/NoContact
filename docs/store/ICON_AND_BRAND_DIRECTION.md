# Icon & Brand Direction - Nosto

## 1. Brand Philosophy: "Nosto"
The brand is built around the concept of "Nosto" (Durgun, Sakin). It is the opposite of chaotic social media or urgent dating apps. It is a quiet room you enter to regulate yourself. 

## 2. Colors to Use
- **Primary:** Sage Green (Calming, nature, growth without force)
- **Backgrounds:** Cream / Off-white (Soft, paper-like, non-glaring)
- **Accents:** Terracotta / Muted Clay (Warmth, human touch, grounding)
- **Text:** Dark Charcoal (Not pure black, to reduce contrast harshness)

## 3. Colors to Avoid
- **Neon or Bright Colors:** No pure red, bright blue, or toxic green.
- **Dating App Colors:** Avoid bright pinks, purples, or gradients that suggest "matching" or "romance."
- **Medical Colors:** Avoid clinical blues or sterile whites.

## 4. App Icon Ideas
The icon should be recognizable but abstract enough not to draw unwanted attention on a home screen.

- **Idea 1 (The Pebble):** A smooth, organic, asymmetrical pebble shape in Sage Green on a Cream background. Represents grounding and stillness.
- **Idea 2 (The Pause):** A very subtle, soft interpretation of a "pause" symbol (II), but with rounded, unequal, organic strokes in Terracotta.
- **Idea 3 (The Horizon):** A minimal, abstract depiction of a calm horizon line separating Cream and Sage, implying a new, quiet day.

## 5. Icon Shapes to Avoid
- **No Broken Hearts:** Avoid cliché breakup imagery. It reinforces trauma rather than healing.
- **No Medical Crosses or Stethoscopes:** This is not a medical app.
- **No Chat Bubbles:** It is not a messaging app.
- **No Stop Signs or Exclamation Marks:** The app should not feel restrictive or alarming.

## 6. Typography Recommendation
- **Store Name:** Use a clean, modern Sans-serif (like Inter or Manrope). No script fonts or overly playful typography.
- **Vibe:** Mature, quiet, self-respecting.
